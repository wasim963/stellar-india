About Us



Welcome to Stellar India


It is our immense pleasure to introduce to you “Stellar India Consultants” as MEP Services Consultants. Stellar India Consultants is a Consultant firm with a highly qualified and professional team of Electrical, HVAC, Plumbing and MEP services in India. The Firm was established in year 2010 under the aegis of Shashidhar Rai.

Stellar India Consultants provide its specialized consultancy services in Electrical, Low Voltage, HVAC, Public Health Engineering (Plumbing, Water and Waste Water Treatment & Management) and Fire Fight ing System along with, environment engineering systems and allied fields.

Stellar India has specialized skills, manpower and expertise in planning of all kinds of building complexes like, Group Housing/ Residential, Hotels, Institutional, Industrial, Townships, Academic, Multiplexes and Mall buildings.


Stellar India Consultant’s contribution to all the projects is from concept design to completion Stage. We have been providing consultancy services for all projects with all government and private clients including Consulting Engineering Firms, Public Works Departments, Development Authorities, and Public-Sector Undertakings of the State & Central Governments in India.

Stellar India will consistently endeavor to multiply its abilities of providing services, meeting international standards and ensure complete customer satisfaction.
